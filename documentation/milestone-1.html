Personal Website Project

Purpose

This project will be a showcase portfolio for my fullstack web skills and professional projects.

Audience

This site is being created for a professional audience, specifically focusing on: hiring managers, recruiters, and startup founders.

Goal

The goal of this project is to give the intended users a point of contact for me regarding employment opportunities. It is also intended to serve as a platform to show my development skills and completed projects.

Persona

Name

Jason Voorhees

Age

36

Profession

Lead developer at Retailer Web Services looking to hire a full time front end developer. Their product is a semi-independent website for small furniture retailers across the country.

Technology

Windows user, Dell Vostro 3681 with Windows 10 and dual monitors at the office. Has an Alienware at home with Ubuntu Linux and a Samsung Galaxy s10e, uses Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox

Attitudes and Behavior

Overworked due to being understaffed, has 3 minutes max to look at a personal portfolio, and less than a minute for resumes. Seeks most potential candidates through LinkedIn and Ziprecruiter

Frustrations and Needs

Looking for at least 2 years of experience with a bachelors in computer science or computer information systems. Also must fit in with the friendly culture of the company. Is ok to hire without degree for lower pay with the right experience and personality. Needs to hire 8 developers before the new year.

Goals

Needs to determine whether candidates have the skills and personality to fit into the company. Brief website visit will let him know if he should continue to phone interview, and after that a zoom call interview.

User Story

"As a power user, I need to know quickly if this person has the experience and skills necessary to move on to the next interview phase."
